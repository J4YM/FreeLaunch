What is Free Launch and how does it differ from other freelancing websites?

Free Launch is a freelancing website that connects clients with freelancers for various tasks and projects. Unlike other platforms, Free Launch emphasizes a commitment to providing a completely free environment for both clients and freelancers, with no fees or charges for using the platform.

How does Free Launch sustain itself without charging fees?

Free Launch operates on a donation-based model. Users have the option to make voluntary contributions to support the platform. These donations help cover operational costs and keep the platform free for all users.

What types of freelance services can be found on Free Launch?

Free Launch accommodates a wide range of freelance services, including but not limited to graphic design, writing, programming, marketing, and virtual assistance. The platform aims to be inclusive, allowing freelancers from various fields to offer their skills.

How does Free Launch ensure quality and reliability in freelancers?

Free Launch incorporates a rating and review system. Clients can provide feedback on freelancers based on their experience, helping build a community-driven reputation system. This system assists clients in making informed decisions when choosing freelancers for their projects.

Are there any restrictions on who can join Free Launch?

Free Launch is open to freelancers and clients worldwide, promoting inclusivity. There are no specific restrictions on geographical location or professional background. Anyone with skills to offer or tasks to be completed is welcome to join the platform and participate in the freelancing community..
